Product

How it works

  • Rules Engine: 7-point eligibility framework validates investor type, KYC, minimum investment, suitability, and classification evidence. Composite AND/OR/NOT logic for complex conditions.
  • Decision Provenance: Every check creates a tamper-evident record with SHA-256 integrity chain. The exact rules + exact input, cryptographically linked.
  • Jurisdiction Templates: Pre-configured for DE (KAGB), LU (CSSF), IE (CBI), EU (ELTIF 2.0). Each rule cites the specific regulation.
  • Compliance Copilot: "Why was this rejected?" ‚Üí plain-English explanation with KAGB ¬ß1 citations. Scenario modeling for rule changes.
  • Annex IV Reporting: Leverage, liquidity buckets, geographic exposure, counterparty risk ‚Äî ~80% of Article 24 data fields.
