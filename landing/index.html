The Problem
Spreadsheets don't survive regulatory examinations.
Most fund managers still check investor eligibility manually. When BaFin asks for the decision trail from 6 months ago, it takes days to reconstruct.

Manual eligibility checks

Compliance officers cross-reference investor type, jurisdiction, KYC status, and minimum investment thresholds in spreadsheets. One missed field = regulatory risk.

No decision audit trail

When a regulator asks "why was this investor approved 6 months ago?", you're reconstructing from emails and memory. That's not compliance — that's hope.

Rule changes break everything

When eligibility criteria change, there's no way to know which existing investors are affected. You find out during the next audit — not before.
